This stores all ride posts
Fields:
- rideId (unique identifier)
- driverId (who posted this ride)
- status (active, full, completed, cancelled)
- route (origin, destination, planned stops, max detour time)
- tripDetails (date, time, seats available, flexibility)
- pricing (price per seat, payment methods accepted, calculated gas cost)
- car (make, model, year, color, photo, license plate)
- postDetails (caption, tags, playlist, engagement metrics)
- riders (array of rider objects with their status and payment confirmation)

This stores ratings and reviews after each trip
Fields:
- reviewId (unique identifier)
- rideId (which trip this review is about)
- reviewerId (who left the review)
- revieweeId (who is being reviewed)
- rating (1-5 stars)
- reviewText (optional written review)
- tags (on-time, friendly, safe-driver, clean-car, etc.)
- helpfulCount (how many found this review helpful)
- createdAt (timestamp)
